Our Standard Aircon Cleaning & Maintenance Service Includes

Optimizing your air conditioner's performance requires several important steps:

Filter Care: Clogged filters restrict airflow, increasing energy consumption and reducing the air conditioner's overall efficiency. Regular cleaning or replacement of air filters is recommended to maintain optimal air quality and improve energy efficiency.

Coil Hygiene: The condenser and evaporator coils harbor dust, dirt, molds, and other contaminants. Cleaning the coils regularly improves the air quality by reducing the pollutants. Coil hygiene is important for energy-efficient and cost-effective cooling.

Drain Line Service: Check and clean the condensate drain line regularly or replace the drain line. Drain line blockages may lead to water leakage and system damage. Condensate drain line service ensures the smooth functioning of your AC

Blower Upkeep: Blower fan and motor maintenance are vital in aircon service. Timely maintenance ensures improved air quality, enhanced efficiency, improved air circulation, and reduced noise levels. By proper blower maintenance, you can ensure the optimized performance of your AC unit.

Electrical System Assessment: Regular electrical component inspection is important for preventative maintenance. Regular inspections help to identify potential problems before they lead to major breakdowns. This also prevents safety hazards like fire, electrical shocks, and other malfunctions.

Refrigerant Level Inspection: An expert technician should check refrigerant levels. Maintaining adequate refrigerant levels is important for an efficient cooling system. Leaking may result in low refrigerant levels and significantly reduce cooling efficiency.

Fin Cleaning and Restoration: Gently clean the fins of the condenser and evaporator coils to remove any obstruction. Straightening the bent fins will improve airflow. Fin cleaning will enhance cooling efficiency, reduce strain on the system, and give the AC unit a longer lifespan.

Ductwork Maintenance: Ductwork maintenance is crucial in aircon service. Dirty ducts will have allergens, dust, mold, mildew, and even pests. Regular maintenance of ductwork will result in improved air quality, enhanced energy efficiency, increased comfort, and reduced noise levels.

System Evaluation: A thorough system evaluation will identify potential issues with AC unit before the unexpected major breakdown occurs. This will include an inspection of the start and shut-off cycles. This will ensure safety, improve energy efficiency, and optimize performance.

General Aircon Cleaning & Maintenance

The air filter will be removed and assessed for any dirt, dust, or debris. Disposable filters will be replaced and washable filter will be cleaned with vacuum or water. Also, front panel and cover, blower wheel, and fan blade will be cleaned for any dirt, debris, or smudges.

Specialized deodorizing or purifying sprays or treatments will be applied to the filter to eliminate odors and kill any bacteria present. This process purifies the air filter, providing optimal air quality and cooling.

Evaporator coil cleaning includes gently removing dust, dirt, pollen, and other contaminants from the evaporator. Extreme cleaning will be done with cleaning solutions to remove grime, mold, and mildew build-up. Indoor drainage tray cleaning will be done to remove debris and any obstructions.

Aircon Repair Service

Vacuuming the drainage pan includes removing any accumulated water from the drainage pan, cleaning debris, and ensuring proper drainage through the drainage pan. Proper lubrication of the motor bearing will be done for optimal performance.

Brushing of the outdoor compressor coil is done to improve the airflow, enhance the cooling efficiency, reduce energy consumption, and prevent overheating. Also, inspect compressor suction and discharge gas pressure.

Check and fix electrical wiring because loose electrical wiring can cause electric shocks, short circuits, and even fire. Also, loose wires can interrupt the flow of the electricity affecting the system efficiency. Loose wiring also can cause intermittent shutdowns.
