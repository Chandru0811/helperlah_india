What is Move In/Out Cleaning ?

Why do you need it?
Move In Cleaning

Move-in cleaning is an intensive property cleaning before new occupants move in. This is to ensure that the new occupants move into an entirely clean and healthy environment. During spotless cleaning, special care is taken to remove any traces of previous tenants.


Move-in cleaning includes deep cleaning of all surfaces including walls, floors, ceilings, countertops, cabinets, etc. Disinfection of frequently touched surfaces including door knobs, light switches, etc. Window, floor, and appliance cleaning, removal of dust, etc. are also included in this process.


Move Out Cleaning

Move-out cleaning is a meticulous cleaning service conducted on a property before the occupants vacate. Move-out cleaning is essential to ensure the property is in a pristine state for the arrival of new tenants.


Move-out cleaning includes thorough cleaning of all surfaces including floors, walls, ceilings, cabinets, etc. Disinfection of frequently contacted surfaces including door knobs, bathroom fixtures, etc. Appliances, floor and window cleaning, and removal of dust and cobwebs are also included.
