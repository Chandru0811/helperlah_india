Housekeeper vs Part time Professional Cleaning Assistant

Are you planning to schedule a cleaner in Singapore? To make a perfect choice, you must know the difference between a housekeeper and a professional cleaning assistant.

Housekeeper

Most of the time stays at the employer’s household and gives ongoing support

You need to provide Visa, monthly salary, and other benefits

Job profiles often include childcare and cooking other than cleaning

Employer’s privacy may be affected due to their in-house stay

Part-time Professional Cleaning Assistant

Visits home as per the prior schedule, no need to stay in the household.

Cleaning will be the primary objective without any additional tasks.

A flexible schedule allows one to work on the hours agreed

Privacy of the employer will not be compromised

What we offer ?

KITCHEN CLEANING

Floor Cleaning and Sink Cleaning: Floor cleaning includes sweeping, mopping, and disinfecting the floor. To ensure your safety, we use only high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning solutions throughout the entire cleaning process.

Cleaning Appliances, Cabinet, and Drawers: Washing the stovetop, refrigerator, oven, and other appliances. Wiping down the cabinet surfaces and drawers for any food particles or spills and to organize items.

Washing the Dishes, Basin, and Emptying the Bin: Washing the dishes and basin with premium cleaning solutions. Rinse and dry the dishes promptly. Emptying and cleaning the bin properly. We won’t prefer hard chemicals.

BATHROOM CLEANING

Decluttering, Dusting, and Cleaning the Floor: Remove unnecessary items from the shelves and floor. Wipe down the surfaces like window sills, shelves, etc. Clean and disinfect the floor with sweep, mop, or vacuum.

Clean the Bathtub, Shower, Sink, and Toilet: Clean the tub or shower walls, and showerhead. Also clean the faucet, countertop, and basin. Clean and disinfect the bowl, exterior, seat, handle, and tank.

Clean Mirrors, Cabinets & Drawers, and Emptying the Bin: Cleaning the mirror includes wiping down the water spots and smudges. Also, clean the exterior and interior of the cabinets and arrange the items. Empty the bin and clean it safely

GENERAL HOME CLEANING

Dusting and Vacuuming: Dust and wipe down all surfaces, including furniture, electronics, and home décor items. Also, clean dirt, stains, soils, and other debris from carpets, rugs, cushions, and upholsteries.

Mopping and Changing the Bed Linen: In general home cleaning, mopping the hard floor including tiles, mosaic, hardwood, and laminates with disinfectant is important. Regularly changing bed linens is essential for maintaining a comfortable sleep environment.

Cleaning Windows and Mirrors: Washing the interior and exterior glass surfaces to remove any dirt, stains, soil, or other debris. Remove any stubborn debris, streaks, stains, and smudges from the mirrors. A gentle cleaning is required for windows and mirrors.

MOVE IN AND MOVE OUT CLEANING

Kitchen and Bathroom Cleaning: Sweep, mop, and disinfect the floor. Clean the surfaces of home appliances. Washing the dishes, basins, and emptying the bin. Clean the floor of the bathroom and scrub the toilets, sinks, and tubs.

Bedrooms and Living Areas: Dusting and cleaning the carpets, floors, and other surfaces. Making the bed and changing the linens. Dust furniture, electronics, and home décor items. Vacuum and clean carpets, rugs, cushions, and upholstery for any dirt or debris.

Windows and Mirror Cleaning: Clean windows, sills, and tracks removing stains and smudges. Also wash the interior and exterior glass surfaces. Clean mirrors by removing the water smudges, stains, and streaks.
