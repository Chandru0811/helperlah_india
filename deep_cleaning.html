What we offer ?

Kitchen Cleaning

Floor Cleaning: Sweeping, mopping, and washing of the floor including the tiles, mosaics, and laminates. Disinfecting the floor using gentle and eco-friendly disinfectants. Deep cleaning also includes cleaning under and behind the appliances.

Cleaning appliances, Countertops, and Cabinets Wiping the surface of the home appliances including refrigerator, oven, stove, etc. Thoroughly clean the counter-tops and disinfect the surfaces. Wiping the cabinets and drawers neatly.

Cleaning sink, basin, and faucets: Deep cleaning the sink, wash basin, and faucets. Disinfecting the sink, basin, and faucets. Cleaning around the faucet base and cleaning the drain are also included in this session.

Bathroom Cleaning

Shower/bathtub, toilet, sink cleaning: Clean surfaces of the shower and bathtub to remove soap scum or any grim. Also clean the shower head, faucet, and other fixtures. Clean and disinfect the toilet's inside and outside, including the bowl, seat, etc. Clean the surface and faucet of the sink.

Cleaning the floor and wall tiles: For best results, sweep or vacuum the floor before mopping. After cleaning, mop the floor thoroughly with a disinfectant cleaner. Scrub the walls with a cleaner to remove dirt and soil.

Cleaning the mirrors: Clean the mirrors using glass cleaners. Use a microfiber cloth to effectively remove dirt, streaks, water smudges, and other stubborn stains. Extra care is taken while cleaning the mirrors to avoid any damage to the mirror surface.

Living Room/Bedroom

Dusting, Vacuuming/Sweeping: Dusting the furniture, electronics, Decoratives, windowsills, blinds/curtains. Vacuuming or sweeping includes carpets, rugs, under and around the furniture, under the bed, etc.

Mopping the Floor and Cleaning the Windows: Mopping the hardwood or tile floors with disinfectant cleaners. Clean any marks or smudges on walls or baseboards. Clean the exterior and interior surfaces of the windows.

Making the bed and Cleaning the Ceiling Lights and Fans: Change sheets, pillowcases, or blankets as needed. Thoroughly clean the ceiling lights and fans for any dust or stains. Regular dusting and cleaning ensure longevity and safety.

Supplementary Services

Mattress Steam Cleaning: You can effectively eliminate dust mites, bacteria, and other microorganisms in the mattress with appropriate steaming. We also can remove stains and unpleasant odors with appropriate steaming. Steaming also helps to sanitize the mattress.

Kitchen Deep Cleaning: The degreasing of the kitchen includes the removal of grease or oil build-up from various surfaces. This includes cleaning the stovetops, oven, countertops, sink, backsplash, and other appliances. Steam cleaning of the kitchen floor is also included in this process.

Carpet & Parquet Care and Stain Removal: Carpet cleaning includes cleaning the carpets, rugs, and upholstery. Parquet care includes varnishing the parquet floor for longevity and maintaining its shine and luster. Tape stain removal is also done with precision.

What we do not cover


Deep cleaning normally doesn’t include cleaning the inside of the appliances, including refrigerator, oven, dishwasher, etc. Yard work, moving heavy furniture, laundry, cleaning inside the cabinets and drawers, and washing windows are not included in deep cleaning.
