Full time Cleaning Assistant vs Part time Professional Cleaner

Are you searching for a cleaning assistant for your Singapore Office? To make a proper choice, you must know the difference between a full-time cleaning assistant and a part-time professional cleaner.

Full-time Cleaning Assistant

Your cleaning assistant will be at your office for full-time to give ongoing support

You need to provide Visa, monthly salary, and other benefits

Job profiles often include responsibilities other than cleaning

Employer’s privacy may be affected due to their full-time job in the office

Part-time Professional Cleaner

Visits office as per the prior schedule, no need to stay full-time in the office.

Cleaning will be the primary objective without any additional tasks.

A flexible schedule allows the person to work on the agreed hours

Privacy of the employer will not be affected

What we offer ?

Reception and Waiting Area Cleaning

A clean and well-maintained reception and waiting area creates a positive and welcoming first impression on our clients and visitors. The reception and waiting area serve as the primary point of contact between an office and its clients and visitors.

We have professionally trained cleaning staff to ensure your reception and waiting area is always clean and shining. This includes dusting and wiping the cabins, sofas, chairs, walls, interior plants, and decor, as well as mopping the floor.

A clean and well-maintained reception and waiting area will enhance brand image and professionalism, client comfort, and employee morale. Regular cleaning and disinfection contribute to a healthy office environment.

Bathroom Cleaning

Maintaining clean and sanitary restrooms in the office is essential in promoting good hygiene practices. Regular cleaning is good for maintaining good hygiene and health. This will certainly improve employee satisfaction. This will reflect positively on the company’s image.

Bathroom cleaning includes cleaning toilets and urinals, sinks and countertops, mirrors, floors, trash removal, ventilation, and odour control. Toilet and urinal cleaning consists of thorough cleaning and disinfecting.

We have a professionally trained team to make your restroom safe and secure. We use only premium cleaning solutions to clean and disinfect the bathroom, and we pay attention to even minute details. A clean and hygienic bathroom will help to build a healthy environment in your workspace.

General Office Cleaning & Dusting

General office cleaning and dusting help provide employees with a healthy and productive environment. Clean and organized offices create a positive impression on visitors, vendors, and clients.

General office cleaning and dusting include dusting all surfaces, chairs, tables, desks, etc., dusting electronics, including computer monitors, keyboards, printers, etc., and dusting ceiling fans, office decor, trophies and shields, air vents, etc.

Surface cleaning including wiping all surfaces with ideal cleaning solutions and disinfecting frequently used surfaces including door handles, light switches, etc. Floor cleaning including vacuuming carpets, runners, and rugs for any dust. Emptying recycling bins also comes under this service.

Breakroom and Kitchen Cleaning

Breakrooms provide a place for employees to take a break from their work, relax, and de-stress. As this is where employees spend time together, clean and hygienic breakrooms will boost the employees' morale.

Kitchen cleaning is crucial for an office to maintain a healthy and productive work environment. A well-organized and well-kept kitchen gives a positive impression on visitors and employees. A clean kitchen is important for food safety and minimizing the spread of germs.

Kitchen cleaning includes surface cleaning, appliance cleaning, floor cleaning, trash removal, and disinfection of the kitchen. Breakroom cleaning includes surface cleaning (tables, chairs, countertops, vending machines, etc.), floor cleaning, trash removal, disinfection, restocking, etc.
