What we offer ?

Clogs, Chokes & Blocks

Obstruction in pipes occurs due to soap scum, hair, food scraps, grease, and other foreign objects. Our expert and skilled plumbers use different techniques to remove the clogs, including snaking, hydro jetting, chemical cleaning, and sometimes pipe replacement.

Chokes are severe blockages that significantly restrict or completely stop the flow of the water. This may be due to a build-up of materials over time or sudden blockage by a large object. Our experienced plumbers accurately identify the blockage and re-establish normal water flow.

Blocks are complete obstructions of the flow of water. This occurs due to severe clogs, tree roots, or due to foreign objects lodged in pipes. Plumbers use snaking, hydro jetting, and chemical cleaning to remove the blocks. In rare cases, pipe replacement takes place.

Water Leakage - Spotting & Repair

If you suspect a water leakage in your home or business, it is advisable to call a qualified plumber, diagnose the problem, and do adequate repairs. Helperlah always offers professional services to save your valuable time and money.

There are a lot of leak detection methods available including visual inspection, acoustic listening, electronic leak detection, dye testing, and thermal imaging to accurately find the leakage and do the necessary repairs to prevent any damage.

Prompt leak detection and repairs will prevent costly water damage to your property. Proper maintenance and repair will reduce water bills and water wastage. Addressing these water leaks promptly will prevent mold growth and other health hazards.

General Pipe Repairs

This includes the repair of leaky pipes caused by age, corrosion, physical damage, and loose joints. Our expert plumbing team quickly identifies the problems and takes appropriate measures to solve the problems including, pipe replacement, joint repairs, or using the pipe clamps.

Installation of water heaters and maintenance are also included in general repairs. Fixing or replacing leaky faucets, toilets, or other fixtures, replacing faulty valves that control water flow, and addressing high or low water pressure were also included in this process.

Our experienced plumbers effectively address a range of plumbing issues, including leaky, clogged, frozen, burst, and corroded pipes. Regular pipe repairs and maintenance will prevent water damage, ensure safety, and maintain plumbing system efficiency.
